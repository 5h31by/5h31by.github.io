My Life in Pictures


This is a photograph of me and my two cousins, my brother, and my sister. This was taken on Easter, and (if I remember correctly) my parents demanded a good picture before we could hunt for eggs.

Here is me from Halloween two years ago. Considering that I made the costume from random things in my house (and Google inspiration), I think it's pretty good.

This picture was taken at my 8th grade graduation. You can easily see the havoc of parents trying very hard to get good pictures, and my classmates trying desperately hard to group together their friends.

Here is some of my friends at the 8th grade dance. Or, to be exact, before we went into the dance. After the dance we looked much worse, and much more depressed.

Here is a picture of me and my siblings making pizzas. We do this every year on Christmas Eve.

Here you can see a picture of me riding a horse and looking like I was having the time of my life.


This photograph was taken at Disneyland, and I have no idea why we were not more excited.
